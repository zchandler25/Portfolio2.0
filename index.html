"LunchBox""

LunchBox is a K3S cluster of Raspberry Pi 4 housed in a 3D printed case that incorporates a network switch, PC fan, Powersupply unit and a small back up HDD. The purpose is to develop a small on-prem appliance that utilizes the cloud when workloads surpass the local resources. one use case is a semi mobile datacenter that can be installed in an RV....just gotta convince the wife.

Thunderdome

"Two CPU's enter, one CPU leave!" Thunderdome is the name of my primary on-prem compute capability. It is 2 Dell R710s, and a Dell R320. In total it has 60 Cores, 168 GB Ram and a combined 10 TB of usable RAID 5 SAS Drives. Inside the box I am using Proxmox as a Type 1 Hypervisor.

SRCR

The Student-Run Cyber Range is designed to expose students to Enterprise IT Environments. The SRCR will train students with industry tools to develop their skills while integrating their experience with a SOC (Security Operations Center). This will be through several avenues, including self-guided discovery, hands-on training, lab exercises, and the use of computing resources typically unavailable to students.
